# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective

Create an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales data and present business insights through data visualization.

## Dataset

Sample Superstore Dataset

## Tool Used

* Power BI

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Summary

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Sales by Category
* Profit by Category
* Sales by Region

### Sales Analysis

* Monthly Sales Trend
* Sales by Segment
* Top 10 Products by Sales

### Profitability Analysis

* Profit by Region
* Discount vs Profit
* Loss-Making Products

## Filters Used

* Region
* Category
* Segment

## Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales.
* Sales varied across regions.
* Top products contributed significantly to revenue.
* Higher discounts often reduced profitability.

## Outcome

Developed an interactive dashboard that converts raw sales data into meaningful business insights using effective data storytelling.
